Note to Employers

This website is not a job listing site you can use. Many such websites allow you to post job ads but this is not one. This site works by using data-mining and business intelligence to find job openings in the most critical sections of the economy. These are real jobs, hard-working, sweat, blood and tears jobs.

We synthesize the entire hiring process based on the data we mine. The data and algorithms we use to do this are open-source.

Job seekers do not apply with individual employers one at a time. Instead, they apply to sectors of the economy where labour is most in demand.

Our approach to completely synthesized, data-driven recruitment removes human bias, prejudice and associated bureaucratic frictions, dead-ends and delays from the hiring process.

For Job Seekers

We aim to inform and empower you. There is nothing worse than idling while you could be earning. This site cuts through the inefficient nature of the job market and puts you in the driver's seat of a sharply tuned and effective machine.

  • Be ready to leave your comfort zone because this work will define you.
  • Be ready to travel and move to another region because not all opportunities are at home.
  • Be ready for doors to open because we distil job requirements down to the absolute minimum and then we give you a clear path to the meeting any remaining requirements such as trade tickets or clearances.
  • Be ready with the right clothing and PPE.
  • Be ready with all your forms completed and ready to go.
  • Be ready with transport to the induction and work-site.
  • Be ready with your accommodation and all other living requirements.
